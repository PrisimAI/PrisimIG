Welcome to PrisimIG!

Let's get you started with a quick tour:

1

Describe Your Image

Type a detailed description of the image you want to create in the text area.

2

Select Options

Choose your preferred AI Model, Size, and Style using the dropdowns.

3

Batch Size & Seed

Adjust the Batch Size to generate multiple images at once. You can also input a Seed for reproducible results.

4

Generate & View

Click the "Generate Image" button. Your creations will appear in the main feed.

5

Remix and Download

Right-click on an image to "remix" its prompt, or click "Download" to save it.
